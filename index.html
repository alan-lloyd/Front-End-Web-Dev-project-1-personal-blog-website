Welcome to Reading Museum Blog!

Reading is a small town in England, about 30 miles west of London, that has had its fair share of history.

Some historical artefacts are there for you to see in Reading Museum. It includes a room with local fossils, stuffed birds, and information how birds are faring locally nowadays, in a more urban environment.

The museum includes things concerning the Roman 'town' of Silchester, now just fields near Reading, that has been studied by archeaologists for over a century.

It also contains an excellent facsimile copy of the 'Bayeux tapestry', which outlined the Norman viewpoint regarding the invasion of England in 1066.

This blogsite will include articles from contributors on interesting things it has to offer you.
