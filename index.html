Welcome to Reading Museum Blog!

Reading is a small town in England, about 30 miles west of London, that has had its fair share of history.

It has a room with local fossils, stuffed birds, and information how birds have been faring locally.

It includes displays concerning the nearby Roman town of Silchester, and also the facsimile copy of the 'Bayeux tapestry'.

This blogsite includes contributions on the many things it has to offer you.
